# NASA Space Apps Challenge 2024

## Overview
Participated in the **NASA Space Apps Challenge 2024** as part of team **Cyborgs**. Out of over 500 teams, we were honored to be among **the top 50** selected for the intense 48-hour onsite regional round.

## Event Details
| Category | Details |
| --- | --- |
| Event | NASA Space Apps Challenge 2024 |
| Organizer | NASA with local regional partners |
| Venue | American International University - Bangladesh (AIUB) |
| Date | October 4-5, 2024 |
| Team | Cyborgs |
| Team Size | 6 members |

## Competition Format
- Preliminary Round: Over 500 teams applied across Bangladesh.
- Onsite Final Round: Top 50 teams were selected for a 48-hour hackathon.
- Goal: Solving complex problems using NASA's open data.

## Results & Media
- Selected as one of the **Top 50 Regional Finalists**.
- Successfully completed a 48-hour intensive hackathon challenge.
